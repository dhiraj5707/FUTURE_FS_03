Gathering around the fire

What started as a pop-up dinner in the woods became a beloved neighborhood spot. We believe in food that tells a story — wood‑fired, vegetable‑forward, and always made with locally sourced ingredients. Every plate is crafted to bring people together, just like the old hearths.

Our open kitchen runs on passion, and our patio is full of laughter. Come as you are, leave satisfied.
